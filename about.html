Welcome to my page, I am Yulong WANG，a PhD student who is currently living in Tokyo.

My research interest focuses on coastal sustainability issues, specifically, focuses on the coastal environment and ecosystem.

Although the total pollutant load control system (TPLCS) of Tokyo Bay had been implemented since 1979, leading to overall improvement of water quality, the hypoxia and anoxia have not been mitigated, and the fish catch still declined continuously. The current research question I am tackling is related to the improvement of the TPLCS, stakeholders' acceptance investigation and environmental feasibility evaluation.
